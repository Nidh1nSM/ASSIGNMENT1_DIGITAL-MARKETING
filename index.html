Amal Jyothi College of Engineering

Strategic Analysis of the Marketing Mix (4Ps)

Executive Summary

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE), Kanjirappally, is a premier central-travancore institution known for its infrastructure and industry-tie-ups. This report analyzes how AJCE leverages the 4Ps to maintain its status as the first self-financing engineering college in Kerala to be managed by a Catholic Diocese.

1. Product: Academic Offerings

Programs & Curriculum

  • B.Tech in emerging fields (AI, Data Science, Robotics).
  • NBA Accredited departments ensuring quality.
  • Strong focus on research and innovation through the Start-up Valley TBI.

Campus Facilities

Equipped with state-of-the-art labs, a massive Central Library, and high-speed Wi-Fi across the 65-acre campus.

2. Price: Value Proposition

AJCE follows a structured fee system regulated by the Kerala Government, complemented by significant scholarship incentives.

3. Place: Location & Accessibility

Strategic Hub: Located in Koovappally, Kanjirappally, along the NH-183, the college serves as a hub for students from across Kerala.

Digital Presence: Beyond the physical campus, AJCE utilizes advanced AES (Academic Enterprise Solution) and digital portals for student services, ensuring learning is accessible 24/7.

4. Promotion: Branding & Communication

AJCE maintains a high-visibility profile through:

  • Events: Hosting national fests like 'Azure'.
  • Social Media: Engaging content on Instagram and YouTube showcasing student life.
  • Alumni Network: Leveraging successful global alumni for word-of-mouth branding.
